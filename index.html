WHAT IS TOUHOU?

Touhou Project (or simply called as Touhou) is an indie Japanese 2D vertical-scrolling shooter video game series mainly based on the genres "Bullet hell" and "Shoot 'em up". All of the games in the series are made by a sole Japanese developer ZUN.

The Shoot 'em up genre is basically controlling the player to move around and shoot the upcoming wave of enemies while dodging their attacks. But when you include the sub-genre "Bullet Hell", it is where you have to dodge a large amount of enemy bullets on the screen.

WHO CREATED TOUHOU?

Jun'ya Ota, mostly known by his alias ZUN, created the Touhou Project series entirely by himself including programming, composing music, designing and writing the story. He works under his company name "Team Shanghai Alice".

ZUN publicly released his first game, "Highly Responsive to Prayers", in 1997 on the PC-98 computer and then he moved to Windows in 2002 to continue making games. He made a total of 18 mainline games and 6 spin-offs and his most recent game is "Touhou 18.5: 100th Black Market" released in Auguest 14, 2022. He is currently working on Touhou 19!

WHAT MAKES TOUHOU POPULAR?

Touhou is relatively popular with thousands of fans wordwide but it's largely popular in Japan. It is mostly because of the games' soundtracks are unique and catchy to hear and the characters, mostly casted with females, are quite adorable and endearing.

ZUN gave his fans the creative freedom to make any content or creations from his work. These kinds of content include song remixes, cosplays, fanarts, memes, animations and many more! Hence, getting his work recognised by people on the internet.

WHAT'S THE LORE OF TOUHOU?

The game's main setting revolves around the World of Gensokyo, which is a magical sealed-off land inspired with elements of old Traditional Japanese culture and Japanese folktales. You can check its map here.

The two main characters that feature in every game are Reimu Hakurei and Marisa Kirisame. They both follow similar objectives; solving an incident as well as finding the culprit who caused it. However, Reimu is more on teaching these culprits a lesson whereas Marisa is more on researching the incident and gaining magic powers.
